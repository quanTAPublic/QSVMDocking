# QSVMDocking
Code / Quantum Kernel and (Encoded) Data for the Research Article "Quantum Kernel Estimation in Support Vector Machines for the Docking of 9-mer Peptides to Immune Receptors" by Luiz Pablo González Dominguez, Zahed Khatooni, Heather Wilson, Gordon Broderick, and Steven Rayan.

The code artefacts were prepared by L.P. González Dominguez and the data has been provided by  Z. Khatooni and H. Wilson of the Vaccine and Infectious Disease Organization (VIDO) at the University of Saskatchewan, Saskatoon, SK, Canada, after encoding and flattening by L.P. González Dominguez. The artefacts are maintained and made publicly available by S. Rayan via this repository of the quanTAPublic GitHub, which is operated by the Centre for Quantum Topology and Its Applications (quanTA) at the University of Saskatchewan.

Please direct questions about this repository to L.P. González Dominguez (qeb408@usask.ca) and S. Rayan (steven.rayan@usask.ca).
